1. Introduction

AI Product Translator ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Shopify application.

Key Points:
  • We do NOT store your product data on our servers
  • API keys are stored in your browser session only
  • Product data is sent to OpenAI API for translation purposes only
  • You maintain full control over your data

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Shopify Store Information: Store URL, shop name, and owner email (provided by Shopify during installation)
  • OpenAI API Key: Your OpenAI API key (stored only in your browser's session storage)
  • App Settings: Your translation preferences (language, tone, model selection)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Product Data: Product titles, descriptions, tags, and SEO metadata (accessed temporarily for translation only)
  • Usage Information: App usage statistics for functionality improvement
  • Session Information: Authentication tokens provided by Shopify

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Customer personal information from your store
  • Payment information or credit card details
  • Order data or transaction history
  • Your customers' email addresses or contact information

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Translation Services: Send product data to OpenAI API for AI-powered translation
  • App Functionality: Enable core features like bulk translation and language management
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • OpenAI API Key: Stored exclusively in your browser's session storage (never transmitted to our servers)
  • App Settings: Stored in your browser's session storage
  • Product Data: NOT stored by us; only temporarily accessed during translation and immediately discarded
  • Session Tokens: Stored in our database for Shopify authentication (encrypted)

4.2 Security Measures

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • No server-side storage of sensitive API keys
  • Secure authentication via Shopify OAuth
  • Regular security audits and updates
Important: Your OpenAI API key is NEVER sent to our servers. It is stored only in your browser and used directly to communicate with OpenAI from your browser session.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to provide translation services. Product data (titles, descriptions, tags) is sent to OpenAI for processing. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy at https://openai.com/privacy

  • Data Sent: Product titles, descriptions, tags, and SEO metadata
  • Purpose: AI-powered translation
  • Retention: According to OpenAI's data retention policy (typically 30 days for API data)

5.2 Shopify

Our app integrates with Shopify and accesses your store data through Shopify's API. Please review Shopify's Privacy Policy at https://www.shopify.com/legal/privacy

5.3 No Other Third Parties

We do NOT share your data with any other third-party services, advertisers, or analytics platforms.

6. Data Retention

  • Session Data: Cleared when you close your browser or log out
  • API Keys: Deleted when you clear browser data (never stored on our servers)
  • Authentication Tokens: Retained for the duration of app installation; deleted upon app uninstallation
  • Product Data: NOT retained by us at any time

6.1 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Uninstall the App: All authentication data will be automatically deleted within 48 hours
  • Request Deletion: Contact us at pes1228@gmail.com to request immediate data deletion
  • Clear Browser Data: Clear your browser's session storage to remove API keys and settings
